This course introduces students to the problems of guiding robots to act intelligently in dynamic, unpredictable environments.  Topics include robot design, kinematics, navigation, control, sensor fusion, and perception under uncertainty.  As the sole instructor for this class, I presented lectures twice a week and led a lab in which students used Turtlebots to perform various locomotion tasks.
